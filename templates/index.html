Company Overview

Technology Ideas is a leading Information and Communication Technology company dedicated to providing innovative, reliable and scalable solutions to businesses, government departments and organizations across Africa and beyond.

Technology Ideas is 100% black owned, since its inception in 2012, the company has grown into a trusted technology partner for clients in various industries, including finance, education, health, logistics, public and private sector.

Our team consists of network specialists, cloud architects and cybersecurity experts with minimum A+, N+, Server+ and CCNA qualifications.
